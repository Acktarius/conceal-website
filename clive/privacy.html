Conceal Live Privacy

Conceal-Live protects your privacy and your communications. Conceal-Live relies on a fully distributed network and offers direct peer-to-peer communications between users. It gives the insurance that all your communications do no transit through any servers.

Conceal-Live offers a secure and private communication environment. All connections are end-to-end encrypted.

Conceal-Live does not require any personal information to create an account and to communicate. No email, no phone number are required. Your account is only stored on your device and contains a pair of public and private keys used to encrypt all your communications. A unique Conceal-Live ID is associated to your account and can be shared and used to communicate with other Conceal-Live users. We offer an optional username management system that allows you to associate a username to your Conceal-Live ID. Usernames are stored in a public blockchain-based registry, thus preserving the distributed aspect of Conceal-Live. Once created, a username cannot be transferred, deleted or modified.

Conceal-Live also offers a free public DHT proxy service to provide a better support of push notifications on mobiles. A TURN server is also available to ease the connectivity for users who are in a private network, in case a peer-to-peer connection can not be established. Both services do not store any logs or personal information and can be deactivated from the application settings if wanted.

Conceal-Live is a universal communication platform with privacy as its foundation. At Conceal-Live, we think that privacy is a primary right on the internet. Everybody should be free not to give its data to corporations to benefit from an essential service on the internet. We know better than anyone else why personal information is sensitive, what our responsibility is to protect it, and what our duties are.
